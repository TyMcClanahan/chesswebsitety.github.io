What is chess?

For those unaware, chess is a board game was made over 15 centuries ago and is still played to this day. There is an absurd amount of depth to a game where the rules can be explained in 60 seconds. The goal is to kill the opponent's king without losing yours first. This is done by moving one piece per turn and each of the six pieces have a different movement pattern. All of this culminates to a highly complex and competitive game that will live on as one of greatest games of all time


What is Lichess.org?

As phenomenal as a game as chess is, it is impossible to always have someone to play with over the board. This is where online chess comes into play, within seconds you can be playing against someone of nearly equal skill level to you anywhere on the globe. There are two main competitors for chess websites these are chess.com and lichess.org. Both are free and great avenues for playing chess but there are a fair amount of premuim features on chess.com that are free on lichess.org like Stockfish Analysis, Unlimited Puzzles, and Lessons. The general consensus is that Lichess is better in the chess community which is why it is my chess website of choice

How were these stats collected?

Lichess has a cool feature where it stores every game that you have played on the website These games can all be exported as a PGN which Excel takes and can interpret as a CSV. This allowed for data on all of my chess games to be easily accessible and this is how I collected all the data for this website.


Why have you made this website

This website was made to scientifically prove that I have not improved in chess at all over approximately 4 years of games.
